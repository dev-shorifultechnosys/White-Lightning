Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

For over three generations, White Lightning Distillery has been at the forefront of craft spirit production. Founded in 1975 by master distiller James Mitchell, our distillery combines time-honored traditions with innovative techniques to create spirits of exceptional quality.

White Lightning Distillery is a craft distillery in Barrhead Alberta, specializing in small-batch spirits made from locally sourced liquor. We take pride in crafting premium vodka and gin, delivering smooth, authentic flavors. As a premium Alberta distillery, our passion for quality ensures every bottle reflects the rich taste of our region. Experience the finest small-batch spirits in Alberta, made with dedication and locally sourced ingredients.

The Art of Distillation

Our state-of-the-art facility combines traditional copper pot stills with modern precision equipment. Every step of our process, from grain selection to bottling, is overseen by our master distillers who bring decades of expertise to their craft.

We believe in transparency and education. Our distillery tours offer an intimate look at how we transform the finest ingredients into exceptional spirits, maintaining the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
